§ 02 · Who tends a garden

It takes more than gardeners.

Gardeners
Do the regenerative work and document it from the field.
Operators
Run the garden, set the roles, and confirm the work.
Evaluators
Verify the record and judge what the impact is worth.
Funders
Resource the work and back the gardens worth growing.
Community
Complete the cycle, accounting for impact where the garden lives.
§ 03 · What we built

We don't rebuild the stack. We compose it.

The front door is an offline-first PWA today, with agentic text and voice coming. Underneath, the stack it runs on:

1Assess
Hats Protocol sets roles and permissions: who can do what.
2Do the work
EAS Attestations capture the Work; the Operator approves.
3Verify
EAS Assessment by an Evaluator, minted as a Hypercert.
4Fund
Octant-style Vaults turn yield into buyback. Karma GAP reports to funders.
Substrate
Gardens V2 conviction voting across cycles, ENS identity, account abstraction for gasless use.
