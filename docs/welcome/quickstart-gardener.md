## Step 3: Your First Task (5 Minutes)

Let's document a simple conservation task. We'll use "Litter Cleanup" as an example.

### 3.1 Select an Action

1. Tap **"Litter Cleanup"** from the home screen
2. Read the instructions
3. Tap **"Start Work"**

### 3.2 Do the Work

Go outside and perform the actual conservation task! For litter cleanup:
- Grab a trash bag
- Find a local park or street
- Collect litter for 5-10 minutes

### 3.3 Document with MDR Workflow

**M**edia ‚Üí **D**etails ‚Üí **R**eview

#### Media: Capture Photos

1. **Take "Before" photo**
   - Show the area before cleanup
   - Make sure litter is visible

## Tips for Success

### üì∏ Photo Tips

- **Always include before/after**: Shows the actual change you made
- **Use good lighting**: Natural daylight works best
- **Same angle**: Makes it easy to compare before/after
- **Include scale**: Show area or objects for size reference

### ‚úçÔ∏è Detail Tips

- **Be specific**: "Removed 3 invasive blackberry bushes" vs "Did some gardening"
- **Accurate metrics**: Count carefully, measure when possible
- **Context matters**: Explain why the work was important
- **Note challenges**: Operators appreciate honesty

### ‚è±Ô∏è Submission Tips

- **Submit promptly**: While the work is fresh in your mind
- **Works offline**: Don't wait for WiFi, submit in the field
- **One task per submission**: Don't combine multiple activities
- **Review before submitting**: Can't edit after submission

## Troubleshooting

### "Can't take photos"

- Grant camera permission in browser settings
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear browser cache

### "Work won't submit"

- Check internet connection (if online)
- Verify all required fields filled
- Try again in a few minutes
- Contact support in [Telegram](https://t.me/+N3o3_43iRec1Y2Jh)

### "Passkey not working"

- Ensure biometrics enabled on device
- Try different browser (Chrome/Safari recommended)
- Check device security settings
