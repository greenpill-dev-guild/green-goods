## Overview

Green Goods is a mobile-first app for documenting regenerative work. As a gardener, you can:

1. **Sign in** with Face ID, Touch ID, or your device biometrics — no passwords or seed phrases needed.
2. **Open an action** in your garden to see what regenerative work is needed.
3. **Document your work** by taking photos, adding details, and submitting in one flow.
4. **Work offline** — your progress is saved and syncs automatically when you reconnect.
5. **Track your submissions** as they move through review and verification.

## Best Practices

- Take clear, well-lit photos that show the before and after of your work
- Fill in all details while the work is fresh — accuracy matters for approval
- If you lose connectivity, keep working — drafts are saved locally and sync when you reconnect
- Check your work dashboard regularly to catch any rejections early and resubmit with corrections
