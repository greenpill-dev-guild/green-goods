# Make an Assessment

<StatusBadge status="Live" />

## What this helps you do

Turn a pile of approved work into one coherent impact statement your garden, funders, and evaluators can actually read. An assessment anchors a reporting window to a domain, a diagnosis, a set of outcomes, and the specific actions that did the work. It is the cleanest way to show progress after an approval spree.

:::note Legacy admin vs. new admin
During the Earth Week activation you may be on the **legacy admin** (left rail shows `Dashboard / Gardens / Assessments / Actions / Endowments`). The assessment wizard lives under **Assessments** in the left rail there, not under a **Hub → Assess** tab. The three wizard steps (Domain & Context → Strategy Kernel → Actions & Harvest), fields, and submit action are the same.
:::

## Before you start

- You are signed in as an **operator** or **evaluator** in the garden.
- The garden has enough **approved work** to tell an honest story — skip this until the queue is cleared.
- You have a rough sense of:
  - The **domain** you are reporting in (must be one this garden runs).
  - A short **diagnosis** of the situation you acted on.
  - One to three **SMART outcomes** you will report against.
  - The **actions** you want to include.
  - The **reporting window** (start and end date).

:::tip
If the assessment button is disabled or the page warns "No permission", you are missing the review role in this garden. The wizard checks `canReviewGarden` before it opens.
:::

## Step-by-step flow

The wizard has three steps: **Domain & Context → Strategy Kernel → Actions & Harvest**. Your progress is saved as a draft as you go, so you can close the tab and resume later.

### Step 1: Open the assessment wizard

1. On the **canvas admin**: open the **Hub** workspace, switch to the **Assess** tab, and click **New assessment** (or go directly to `/hub/assess/create`).
2. On the **legacy admin**: open **Assessments** in the left rail and click **Create assessment**.
3. Pick the garden if you haven't already. The wizard refuses to open without one.

<img src="/img/screenshots/admin-garden-impact.png" alt="Garden Impact tab — hypercert highlights, routed yield, and the Assessments entry" style={{maxWidth: '100%', borderRadius: '8px', border: '1px solid var(--ifm-color-emphasis-300)'}} />

> From a garden's detail page, switch to **Impact** and click **View Assessments** (or **Assessments** on the left rail for a global view). From there, **New Assessment** opens the three-step wizard: **Domain & Context → Strategy Kernel → Actions & Harvest**.

### Step 2: Fill Domain & Context

1. Pick the **Domain** this assessment reports against. Only domains enabled on this garden are selectable.
2. Enter the **title** — keep it stable enough that you will still recognise it in six months.
3. Enter a short **description** of what the assessment covers.
4. Enter the **location** (specific enough that a reader can place it on a map).
5. Click **Continue**.

### Step 3: Build the Strategy Kernel

1. Enter the **diagnosis** — one or two paragraphs on what was happening before the work started.
2. Choose the **Cynefin phase** that matches the situation (clear, complicated, complex, chaotic, or confusion). This helps a reader read the assessment with the right frame.
3. Add one or more **SMART outcomes**. Each outcome needs a description, a metric, and a numeric target. Keep them honest — "15 trees alive after 6 months" beats "trees planted."
4. Select the **SDG targets** that apply. Don't blanket-select — pick what the work actually contributes to.
5. Click **Continue**.

### Step 4: Pick Actions & Harvest

1. Select the **actions** to include in this assessment. Only actions run in this garden appear.
2. Set the **reporting period** — start date and end date. Make it match the work you selected.
3. Click **Submit assessment** and sign the transaction in your wallet.
4. Wait for the transaction to confirm. The wizard stays on this step until it does.

## How to know it worked

- A success toast appears: *"Assessment submitted — Your assessment has been recorded on-chain."*
- The wizard closes and you land on the garden **Impact → Assessments** view.
- The new assessment appears in the grid with its title, domain badge, and reporting date range.
- Clicking a card opens the attestation on EAS Explorer, where you can verify the decoded fields independently.

### Behind the scenes

Every assessment is a single EAS attestation with your domain, diagnosis, SMART outcomes, SDG targets, reporting window, and the selected action UIDs. The assessment attestation is what a downstream Hypercert references when it packages the work for funders.

## If something goes wrong

- **"No permission"** on the wizard. You are missing `canReviewGarden`. Ask the garden owner to add the evaluator role.
- **Domain field missing options.** The garden doesn't have that domain enabled. Fix the garden settings first, or pick a domain the garden actually runs.
- **"Incomplete form"** toast. The wizard highlights the failing field — scroll to the red label, fill it in, and retry.
- **"We could not submit the assessment."** The form data didn't assemble into a valid payload — usually a missing garden reference. Reload the page and re-enter the wizard from the cockpit.
- **Transaction cancelled.** You cancelled in your wallet. Click **Retry submission**.
- **Transaction failed.** Check the inline error card. Common causes: wrong chain, insufficient gas, or a stale signer. Fix the cause and click **Retry submission**. If you need to edit the form, click **Edit details** instead.
- **"Draft backup unavailable"** info toast. Your draft could not be saved to local storage, but submission still works — just don't close the tab mid-wizard.
