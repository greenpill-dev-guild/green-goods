# Approve Work

<StatusBadge status="Live" />

## What this helps you do

Clear a pending work submission in your garden: look at the evidence, approve or reject it, and leave feedback the gardener can act on. Approvals turn into on-chain attestations that feed later assessments, so consistency here pays off downstream.

:::note Legacy admin vs. new admin
During the Earth Week activation you may be on the **legacy admin** (left rail shows `Dashboard / Gardens / Assessments / Actions / Endowments`). The review surface there lives inside each garden's detail page under a **Work** tab, not inside a standalone **Hub** workspace. The evidence, approve, and reject controls behave the same — the nav path to reach them differs.
:::

## Before you start

- You are signed in to Green Goods admin and have the **Operator** or **Evaluator** role in the garden.
- There is at least one **pending** submission in the work queue.
- You are online — approvals and rejections need a wallet signature.

:::tip
If you are not sure whether you have review permissions, open any pending submission. If you only see the submission details and no approve/reject controls, you don't have the role yet — ask the garden owner to add you.
:::

## Step-by-step flow

### Step 1: Open the work queue

1. On the **canvas admin**: open the **Hub** workspace from the left rail — the default view lands on **Work**, pending across every garden you can review. Use the garden picker to narrow.
2. On the **legacy admin**: open **Gardens** in the left rail, pick your garden, then switch to the **Work** tab inside the garden detail view.
3. Either way, filter to **Pending** to see what still needs a decision.

<img src="/img/screenshots/admin-work-queue.png" alt="Hub work queue — pending submissions ready for review" style={{maxWidth: '100%', borderRadius: '8px', border: '1px solid var(--ifm-color-emphasis-300)'}} />

> The queue only shows submissions in the **Pending** state. Approved and rejected items move to the history view.

### Step 2: Open one submission

1. Click a submission. The Work Detail surface opens with the evidence on the left and the review controls on the right.
2. Scroll through the media (photos, audio notes) and read the submission details.
3. Cross-check the gardener's numbers or notes against the action's requirements.

<img src="/img/screenshots/admin-work-detail.png" alt="Work Detail — media evidence on the left, review form on the right" style={{maxWidth: '100%', borderRadius: '8px', border: '1px solid var(--ifm-color-emphasis-300)'}} />

> The status badge at the top of the page says **Pending** until you act.

### Step 3: Approve or reject

You'll see the review form on the right (or below, on narrow screens).

**To approve:**

1. Add a short verification note if it helps the next reviewer stay consistent.
2. Click **Approve**.
3. Sign the transaction in your wallet when prompted.

**To reject:**

1. Explain exactly what is wrong — which field, which photo, which count — and what the gardener should change.
2. Click **Reject**.
3. Sign the transaction.

:::tip
Be specific in rejection feedback. *"Photo is too dark to verify tree count — retake in daylight and resubmit against the same action"* will land much better than *"insufficient evidence."*
:::

### Step 4: Confirm the status update

1. After the transaction confirms, the status badge flips to **Approved** or **Rejected**.
2. The submission drops out of the pending queue.
3. You can check it in the Hub **History** view if you want to verify.

## How to know it worked

- The status badge on the submission shows **Approved** (green) or **Rejected** (red), with the matching icon.
- The submission no longer appears in the **Pending** Hub queue.
- The gardener sees the new status (and your feedback, on a rejection) in their Work Dashboard.
- For approvals, an on-chain attestation is recorded linking the work UID to your decision.

### Behind the scenes

Approvals create two linked attestations: one for the work itself and one for your review decision. That pair is what downstream assessments and Hypercerts reference when they prove the work happened and passed review.

## If something goes wrong

- **No Approve/Reject buttons visible.** You don't have the review role in this garden. Ask the garden owner.
- **"Select a garden" style errors.** The garden didn't resolve from the submission — reload the page and open the submission from the queue again.
- **Transaction cancelled.** You cancelled in your wallet. Nothing happens on-chain. Try again when you are ready.
- **Transaction failed.** Check the wallet error. Common causes: insufficient gas, wrong chain, or the garden's permissions contract changed. Switch chain, top up, and retry.
- **Evidence is inconclusive.** Reject with a specific request ("add a second photo showing the full bed") rather than guessing. It is faster to let the gardener resubmit than to approve a weak record you will regret at assessment time.
- **You clicked the wrong decision.** Approvals and rejections are recorded on-chain and can't be flipped in place. If you rejected valid work, ask the gardener to resubmit; if you approved bad evidence, flag the case to the garden owner so it can be annotated in the next assessment.
