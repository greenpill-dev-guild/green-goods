# Earning Badges & Rewards

## Overview

Every approved work submission creates a permanent, verifiable record on the blockchain. Over time, these records build your on-chain reputation — a portable history of verified impact that follows you across gardens and platforms.

{/* IMAGE PLACEHOLDER: Gardener profile showing badge collection and attestation history (800x400) */}

This page covers how to track your submission status, verify your attestation records, and understand how consistent contributions build your reputation.

## How It Works

### Tracking Your Work Status

After submitting work through the MDR workflow, your submission moves through these stages:

- **Uploading/Queued** — Submission is still syncing (common in offline/low-connectivity conditions)
- **Pending** — Waiting for operator review
- **Approved** — Work validated; an approval attestation has been created on-chain
- **Rejected** — Work requires correction and resubmission (check operator feedback)

### Verifying Your Attestations

Every approval creates an EAS attestation — a permanent, cryptographically signed record. You can verify your attestations independently:

### Building Reputation

Your attestation history builds an on-chain reputation that is:

- **Portable** — Your attestations exist on-chain and can be verified by any platform, not just Green Goods
- **Permanent** — Once attested, your verified work record cannot be altered or removed
- **Composable** — Other protocols and applications can read your attestation history to assess your impact track record

## Best Practices

- Check your work dashboard regularly to catch rejections early — resubmit with corrections promptly
- Keep a record of your attestation UIDs for your own portfolio or grant applications
- Consistent, high-quality submissions build stronger reputation faster than volume alone
- If a submission stays in "Pending" for an extended period, check with your garden operator
