# Join a Garden

<StatusBadge status="Live" />

## What this helps you do

Join the garden where your work belongs so its actions appear in the app and your submissions can be reviewed by the right operators.

Open gardens can be joined directly. Restricted gardens do not show a join button until an operator invites or adds you.

## Before you start

- Sign in with the passkey or wallet address you plan to keep using.
- Make sure you are online. Joining a garden is an on-chain action.
- If you are using an external wallet, keep a little gas available on the target chain.
- If someone sent you a public garden link, open **Join this Garden** and sign in when prompted. After login, the app takes you to the authenticated garden page.

## How to know it worked

- The garden appears in your Profile gardens list.
- The garden page no longer shows the join action for your account.
- The Work tab shows actions you can submit against, if the operator has active actions configured.
- If the app still looks unchanged right after signing, refresh once. The transaction may have confirmed before the indexer caught up.

## If something goes wrong

- **No Join button.** The garden may be restricted, you may already be a member, or you may be signed out.
- **Transaction fails.** Check the wallet message. Common causes are the wrong chain, no gas for an external wallet, or a garden that is no longer open for joining.
- **Joined but still not showing.** Refresh the app. The join is optimistic first and then confirmed by the garden indexer.
- **Wrong garden.** Contact the operator before submitting work. Your submissions should be tied to the garden that will review them.

## Best practices

- Confirm the garden location and operator before joining if several gardens have similar names
- When joining a restricted garden, reach out to the operator directly and share the address you use in Green Goods
- You can belong to multiple gardens at once — each garden tracks your contributions independently
- Check a garden's active actions before joining to make sure it matches your interests and availability
