# Joining A Garden

<StatusBadge status="Live" />

## Overview

Gardens are community hubs for regenerative work, each focused on a specific bioregion or initiative. As a gardener, you join a garden to access its actions, document your work, and build a verifiable contribution history. On first sign-in, Green Goods prompts you to join the Community Garden so you can get moving quickly, but you can also skip that prompt and join later from your profile.

## Best Practices

- Start with the Community Garden to learn the MDR submission workflow before joining specialized gardens
- When joining a restricted garden, reach out to the operator directly to express your interest and experience
- You can belong to multiple gardens at once — each garden tracks your contributions independently
- Check a garden's active actions before joining to make sure it matches your interests and availability
