# Garden Payouts

<StatusBadge status="Implemented (activation pending deployment)" />

## Overview

Gardens can compensate gardeners for verified regenerative work through two mechanisms: vault distributions (managed by operators) and cookie jars (self-service withdrawals within preset limits). Both systems are funded by deposits into the garden's on-chain treasury.

## How It Works

### Vault Distributions

Garden vaults hold assets in yield-bearing positions. Operators manage distributions from vault funds to compensate gardeners, fund community initiatives, or reinvest in garden operations.

  steps={[

### Cookie Jars

Cookie jars provide lightweight, recurring payouts with preset limits. If your garden has an active cookie jar, you can withdraw directly without waiting for operator approval.

## Best Practices

- Keep your work submissions consistent and high-quality — contribution history is a key factor in operator-managed distributions
- Check cookie jar withdrawal windows regularly to avoid missing available claims
- If a cookie jar shows zero available, check whether you have already claimed for the current interval
- Ask your garden operator about the payout policy so you know what to expect and when
