# Earning Badges & Recognition

<StatusBadge status="Live" />

## Overview

Evaluator credibility is built through a track record of thorough, accurate impact verification. Every assessment you contribute to and every attestation chain you verify adds to your on-chain history. Funders and other stakeholders rely on evaluator reputation to gauge the trustworthiness of impact claims.

**What contributes to your reputation:**

- **Verification volume and accuracy** — how many attestation chains you have verified and how consistently your findings hold up
- **Assessment contributions** — your participation in garden assessments, especially those that led to successful funding
- **Cross-garden evaluation** — evaluating across multiple gardens demonstrates breadth and independence
- **Methodology documentation** — published evaluation frameworks that others can review and build on

## Best Practices

- Maintain evaluation independence — avoid conflicts of interest by disclosing any affiliations with gardens you evaluate
- Document your methodology so that your results are reproducible and your reasoning is transparent
- Build evaluation history across multiple gardens to demonstrate breadth and reduce bias perception
- Share your evaluation history when seeking new evaluation opportunities or applying for funding analysis roles
- Stay current with evolving impact frameworks and attestation standards to maintain evaluation quality
