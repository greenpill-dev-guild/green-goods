# Funding A Garden

Green Goods makes it simple to fund regenerative communities through **vault deposits** and **Hypercert purchases**. Your capital directly supports verified impact work.

## How Funding Works

Funding in Green Goods flows through two primary mechanisms:

### Vault Deposits

**Octant Vaults** are impact-vault deposit contracts attached to each garden. When you deposit ERC-20 tokens:

1. Your deposit adds principal to the garden endowment
2. Strategies may generate yield in the background while depositor claim value stays flat by design
3. When harvested, yield is split three ways:
   - **Cookie Jar** — Petty cash for gardeners' daily operations
   - **Hypercert Fractions** — Purchasing impact certificate fractions
   - **Community Endowment** — Long-term community treasury

Your principal stays in the vault, and harvested yield funds garden operations through protocol routing rather than depositor share appreciation. You can withdraw your deposit at any time.

### Hypercert Purchases

**Hypercerts** are tokenized impact certificates that represent verified regenerative work. When you purchase a Hypercert fraction:

- You directly fund the community that created the verified impact
- You receive an ERC-1155 token representing your share of the impact claim
- The purchase is transparent and verifiable on-chain

## Getting Started

1. **Browse active gardens** on the [Green Goods Dashboard](https://admin.greengoods.app)
2. **Review verified impact** — Each garden shows its attestation history, work volume, and approval rates
3. **Deposit into a vault** or **purchase Hypercert fractions** to fund the work you believe in
4. **Track your impact** — Monitor routed yield, garden health, and funded outcomes over time
