## What this helps you do

Endow a garden by depositing supported assets into its vault. The vault is designed to preserve your principal while yield supports the garden. You receive a withdrawable position that you can remove later.

The UI still uses the word **Deposit** inside the vault dialog and Treasury drawer. In these docs, **Endow** means making that vault deposit.

There are two entry points:

- **Public `/fund` page.** Good for shared links and first-time support. It lets you choose **Endow** and deposit by wallet.
- **Authenticated app Treasury drawer.** Best when you are already signed in and want to see your active position in the same place.

## Before you start

- A connected wallet or passkey account.
- A supported token balance on Arbitrum.
- A little Arbitrum gas if you use an external wallet. Passkey or smart-account actions may be sponsored when that support is configured.
- The garden you want to endow.
- A live internet connection.

:::tip
Endowments carry smart contract, token, yield, provider, and wallet recovery risk. Only endow what you are comfortable managing through this flow.
:::

## Step-by-step flow

### Option A: Endow from the public `/fund` page

Use this when you are coming from a public garden link.

1. Open `/fund`.
2. Pick the garden you want to support.
3. Click **Support this Garden**.
4. Choose **Endow**.
5. Choose **Wallet**. If you are not connected yet, connect when prompted.
6. In the vault dialog, pick the asset if more than one is available.
7. Enter the amount and click **Deposit**.
8. Sign the transaction and wait for confirmation.

### Option B: Endow from the authenticated app

Use this when you want to see the full Treasury drawer.

1. Sign in to the Green Goods app.
2. Open **Home**, then open the garden.
3. Tap **Treasury** in the top bar.
4. Stay on the **Endowments** tab.
5. Review the overview, including asset, net deposited, and depositor count.
6. Pick the asset if more than one vault is available.
7. Enter the amount. Use **Max** only if you want to use the whole wallet balance for that asset.
8. Check the estimated shares preview.
9. Tap **Deposit** and sign the transaction.
10. Wait for confirmation before closing the drawer.

## How to know it worked

- The amount input clears or the dialog closes after success.
- Your wallet history shows the vault deposit transaction on Arbitrum.
- In the Treasury drawer, **Active deposits** shows your asset, available balance, and shares after the app refreshes.
- The Treasury overview updates after indexing catches up.

## If something goes wrong

- **No vault or Treasury button.** The garden has not enabled a vault yet.
- **Connect wallet prompt appears.** Connect the wallet or passkey account that should hold the endowment position.
- **Offline warning.** Reconnect and retry.
- **Amount exceeds wallet balance.** Enter a smaller amount or use **Max**.
- **Invalid decimal input.** Match the token's decimal precision.
- **Transaction cancelled.** Nothing was deposited. Try again when ready.
- **Transaction failed.** Check the wallet error, token balance, chain, and Arbitrum gas.
- **Still stuck.** Use the Green Goods Telegram support link in the docs footer.
