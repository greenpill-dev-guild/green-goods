# Earning Recognition

As a funder, your contributions to regenerative communities are tracked on-chain and build a **verifiable reputation** across the Green Goods ecosystem.

## How Recognition Works

### On-Chain Funding History

Every vault deposit and Hypercert purchase creates a permanent, verifiable on-chain record. Your funding history is:

- **Transparent** — Anyone can verify your contributions
- **Portable** — Your reputation travels across gardens and platforms
- **Cumulative** — Consistent funding builds a stronger track record

### Funder Role

When you deposit into a garden vault, you receive the **Funder hat** via Hats Protocol. This role:

- Identifies you as a garden supporter
- Grants access to funder-specific dashboards and analytics
- Enables participation in governance decisions about fund allocation

### Impact Attribution

Hypercert fractions provide direct attribution to specific impact claims. When you hold a fraction of a Hypercert, you're recognized as a backer of that verified impact — from the field work photos to the final assessment.

## Future Recognition Features

- **Unlock Protocol badges** — Token-gated credentials for funding milestones
- **Cross-garden recognition** — Reputation that spans multiple communities
- **Funder leaderboards** — Transparent rankings of impact backers
