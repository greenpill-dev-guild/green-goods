# Earning Recognition

As a funder, you already create an **on-chain funding history** when you deposit or participate in certificate-backed flows. What is still evolving is the product layer that turns that history into clearer recognition, badges, or portable reputation views.

## How Recognition Works

### On-Chain Funding History

Every vault deposit and Hypercert purchase creates a permanent, verifiable on-chain record. Your funding history is:

- **Transparent** — Anyone can verify your contributions
- **Portable** — Your reputation travels across gardens and platforms
- **Cumulative** — Consistent funding builds a stronger track record

### What Exists Today

- **Permanent funding records** — Deposits and related transactions are visible on-chain
- **Garden-level attribution** — Your support can be tied back to a specific garden and its treasury activity
- **Certificate-linked history** — Where Hypercert flows are in use, support can be linked to a more specific impact bundle

### What Is Still Taking Shape

- Dedicated funder badges
- Cross-garden reputation views
- Cleaner public profiles for capital allocators
- Opinionated leaderboards or milestone systems

## Future Recognition Features

- **Unlock Protocol badges** — Token-gated credentials for funding milestones
- **Cross-garden recognition** — Reputation that spans multiple communities
- **Funder leaderboards** — Transparent rankings of impact backers
