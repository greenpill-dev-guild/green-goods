## What this helps you do

Remove part or all of your existing garden endowment from the public `/fund` page. The UI calls this action **Withdraw** because it withdraws principal from the vault back to your wallet.

The primary path is **Manage Endowments** on `/fund`. The authenticated app Treasury drawer can still show the same kind of position for signed-in app users, but funders do not need to find the old admin surface to recover an endowment.

## Before you start

- Use the same wallet that holds the vault shares.
- Confirm you have an active endowment in at least one Garden vault.
- Make sure you are online.
- Keep a little Arbitrum gas available if you use an external wallet. Passkey or smart-account actions may be sponsored when that support is configured.

:::tip
The available balance shown in Manage Endowments is the amount you can remove now. It may differ from the original deposit because the vault preview uses the current share-to-asset rate.
:::

## Step-by-step flow

### Step 1: Open Manage Endowments

1. Open `/fund`.
2. Scroll to **Gardens accepting support this season**.
3. Select **Manage Endowments**.
4. Connect the same wallet you used to endow.

### Step 2: Find your active endowment

1. Review the totals under **What you've supported**.
2. Find the Garden group you want.
3. Open the asset row you want to remove from.
4. Check **Endowed**, **Shares**, and **Available if needed**.
5. If the panel is empty, this wallet does not currently hold an indexed endowment position.

### Step 3: Enter the amount

1. Type the amount to remove.
2. Tap **Max** if you want to remove the full available balance.
3. Fix any decimal or balance error shown under the field.
4. The review button enables once the amount is valid.

### Step 4: Confirm and sign

1. Select **Review withdrawal**.
2. Read the inline confirmation.
3. Confirm and sign in your wallet.
4. Wait for the transaction to finish.

## How to know it worked

- The amount input clears after success.
- The panel refreshes after success.
- The row's available amount drops by the amount you removed.
- If you removed the full balance, the row disappears after refresh.
- Your wallet history shows the withdrawal transaction on Arbitrum.
- The asset returns to your wallet on Arbitrum.

## If something goes wrong

- **No endowments appear.** You may have already removed the endowment, used a different wallet, or the indexer may still be syncing.
- **Offline warning.** Reconnect and retry.
- **Review withdrawal stays disabled.** The amount is zero, exceeds the available amount, or has a decimal error.
- **Inline confirmation is open.** Finish the wallet signature flow or cancel and retry.
- **Transaction cancelled.** Nothing was removed. Try again when ready.
- **Transaction failed.** Check the wallet error, chain, gas, and whether the vault can satisfy the requested amount. Try a smaller amount if needed.
- **Still stuck.** Use the Green Goods Telegram support link in the docs footer.
