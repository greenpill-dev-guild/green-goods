## What this helps you do

Remove part or all of your existing garden endowment from the authenticated Treasury drawer. The UI calls this action **Withdraw** because it withdraws principal from the vault back to your wallet.

The public `/fund` page supports Donate and Endow only. To remove an endowment, sign in to the app and open the garden's Treasury drawer.

## Before you start

- Use the same wallet or passkey account that holds the vault shares.
- Confirm you have an active endowment in this garden.
- Make sure you are online.
- Keep a little Arbitrum gas available if you use an external wallet. Passkey or smart-account actions may be sponsored when that support is configured.

:::tip
The available balance shown in the drawer is the amount you can remove now. It may differ slightly from the original deposit because the vault preview uses the current share-to-asset rate.
:::

## Step-by-step flow

### Step 1: Open the Treasury drawer

1. Sign in to the Green Goods app.
2. Open **Home**, then open the garden you endowed.
3. Tap **Treasury** in the top bar.
4. Stay on the **Endowments** tab.

### Step 2: Find your active endowment

1. Scroll to **Active deposits**.
2. Find the asset row you want to remove from.
3. Check **Available balance** and **My shares**.
4. If **Active deposits** is empty, this wallet does not currently hold a position in this garden's vault.

### Step 3: Enter the amount

1. Type the amount to remove.
2. Tap **Max** if you want to remove the full available balance.
3. Fix any decimal or balance error shown under the field.
4. The **Withdraw** button enables once the amount is valid.

### Step 4: Confirm and sign

1. Tap **Withdraw**.
2. Read the confirmation dialog.
3. Confirm and sign in your wallet.
4. Wait for the transaction to finish.

## How to know it worked

- The amount input clears after success.
- The row's **Available balance** drops by the amount you removed.
- If you removed the full balance, the row disappears from **Active deposits** after refresh.
- Your wallet history shows the withdrawal transaction on Arbitrum.
- The asset returns to your wallet on Arbitrum.

## If something goes wrong

- **Active deposits is empty.** You may have already removed the endowment, used a different wallet, or opened the wrong garden.
- **Offline warning.** Reconnect and retry.
- **Withdraw button stays disabled.** The amount is zero, exceeds available balance, or has a decimal error.
- **Confirmation dialog is open.** Finish the wallet signature flow or close the dialog and retry.
- **Transaction cancelled.** Nothing was removed. Try again when ready.
- **Transaction failed.** Check the wallet error, chain, gas, and whether the vault can satisfy the requested amount. Try a smaller amount if needed.
- **Still stuck.** Use the Green Goods Telegram support link in the docs footer.
