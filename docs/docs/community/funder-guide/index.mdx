# Funder Guide

<StatusBadge status="Live" />

## What this helps you do

This is the funder path for Earth Week: deposit into a garden vault, then know exactly where to withdraw your position later.

The public `/fund` page is deposit-only. To see your active position or withdraw, use the authenticated app and open the garden's Treasury drawer.

## Good funding habit

Before depositing, open the garden and look at its recent approved work. After depositing, check the Treasury drawer so you know where your position and withdrawal controls live.
