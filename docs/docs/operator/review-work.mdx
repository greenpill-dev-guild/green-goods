## Overview

When gardeners submit work through the MDR workflow, it enters your review queue. As an operator, you validate the evidence, approve or reject submissions, and provide feedback. Approved work creates permanent on-chain attestations via EAS and triggers Karma GAP impact attestations automatically.

## Best Practices

- Review submissions within 24-48 hours to keep gardeners engaged and motivated
- Be specific in rejection feedback — "photo is too dark to verify tree planting" is better than "insufficient evidence"
- Use batch approval when multiple submissions clearly meet the same action's standards
- Keep notes on edge cases so you maintain consistent review standards over time
- Check that attestation linkage is correct after approving — the work UID and approval refUID should match
