## Overview

A garden is your community hub for regenerative work. Creating one sets up an on-chain identity (ERC-6551 Tokenbound Account) that can hold assets, manage members, and coordinate impact. You will need a garden name, description, location, and a plan for your initial membership.

**Required inputs:**

- Garden name and short description
- Location and banner/media assets
- Joining policy (open vs restricted)
- Initial member/role setup plan

## Best Practices

- Choose a clear, descriptive garden name that reflects the bioregion or community focus
- Set your joining policy thoughtfully — open gardens grow faster, restricted gardens maintain tighter quality control
- Verify that garden metadata renders correctly in both admin and client before sharing with your community
- Create your first action immediately after garden creation to give gardeners something to work on
- Confirm that correct operator/admin permissions are in place before inviting members
