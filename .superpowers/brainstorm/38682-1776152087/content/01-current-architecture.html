Notable Gaps vs Your Mental Model

  • No dedicated "LeftSheet" or "RightSheet" — a single SideSheet component takes a side prop ("left" | "right")
  • BottomSheet is mobile-only, not a persistent transparent container — it's a glass-surfaced modal with drag-to-dismiss
  • NavigationBar sits outside the sheet taxonomy — it's a fixed dock, not a "bottom sheet"
  • TopContextBar is the only truly transparent container — everything else has surface treatment
  • Deprecated FloatingToolbar still exported alongside its replacement (NavigationBar)
