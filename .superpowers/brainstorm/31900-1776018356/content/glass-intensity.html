Liquid Glass Intensity

How much translucency and vibrancy should surfaces have? This affects cards, sheets, navigation bars, and overlays.

Hub Dashboard
Mostly opaque surfaces with hint of blur

A. Subtle Glass

Surfaces are 80-90% opaque with gentle blur. Clean and professional. Background peeks through just enough to create depth without distraction. Think: Linear, Vercel dashboard.

Hub Dashboard
Balanced translucency, visible background

B. Balanced Glass

Surfaces are 45-60% opaque with stronger blur and saturation boost. The workspace color clearly shows through. Think: macOS Sonoma window chrome, iOS Control Center.

Hub Dashboard
Heavy translucency, strong color bleed

C. Heavy Glass

Surfaces are 20-30% opaque with intense blur. Strong iOS 26 / visionOS vibes. Beautiful but can reduce text contrast and readability. Think: visionOS, Apple Liquid Glass (iOS 26).
