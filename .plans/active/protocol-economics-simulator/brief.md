# Protocol Economics Simulator

Expand the Revenue Explorer from a revenue-only calculator into a full protocol economics simulator that models expenses, treasury runway, retro compensation, and token upside for contributors, operators, and gardeners.

**Why**: Green Goods wants to position itself as a rewarding tool where users see upside comparable to core team or investors. The current explorer only answers "where does money come from?" — it needs to also answer "do we survive?", "how do we compensate early contributors?", and "what's in it for participants?"
