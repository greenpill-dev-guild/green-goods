# Code of Conduct

Green Goods is built in public by contributors with different backgrounds, locations, and levels of experience. We expect collaboration to stay respectful, concrete, and focused on the work.

## Expected Behavior

- Be welcoming to new contributors and clear about project context.
- Give feedback on the work, not the person.
- Ask for clarification before assuming intent.
- Respect maintainers' scope, review, and security boundaries.
- Credit contributors and avoid presenting others' work as your own.

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discriminatory language
- Sexualized attention or unwelcome personal comments
- Publishing private information without permission
- Repeated disruption of issues, pull requests, meetings, or community spaces
- Retaliation against someone who reports a concern in good faith

## Scope

This code applies in repository issues, pull requests, docs, meetings, chat spaces, and any other Green Goods project venue.

## Reporting

Report conduct concerns to project maintainers through the most appropriate private channel available to you. If a report includes sensitive security details, use `security@greengoods.app`.

Maintainers may remove comments, close threads, limit participation, or escalate concerns to Greenpill Dev Guild governance when needed.
